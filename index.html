FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How long does it take to build my website?
Most websites are delivered in 3–5 business days. Complex e-commerce stores may take 7–10 days. We always provide a clear timeline before starting, and stick to it.
Do you provide hosting and domain?
Yes! Our Business Plan includes a free domain and 1 year of hosting. After the first year, renewal costs are very affordable (typically ₹2,500–₹4,000/year).
Will my website rank on Google?
Every website we build includes technical SEO fundamentals — fast load speeds, proper meta tags, schema markup, and clean code. Ranking also depends on your content and local competition, but we give you the strongest possible foundation from day one.
Can I update the website content myself?
Absolutely. We can build on WordPress or provide a simple CMS so you can update text, images, and blog posts without any coding knowledge. No tech skills required.
What if I'm not happy with the design?
We offer unlimited revisions until you're 100% satisfied. We also show you a prototype before development starts — so there are absolutely no surprises at the end.
Do you work with businesses outside Bhopal?
Yes! While we're based in Bhopal, we work with clients across India. Everything is handled remotely via WhatsApp and video calls — smooth, fast, and hassle-free.
